VPN Benefits and Limitations

Benefits:
- Encrypts internet traffic, protecting data from hackers and surveillance.
- Masks IP address, enhancing privacy and allowing access to geo-restricted content.
- Prevents ISP tracking and throttling.
- Useful on public Wi-Fi networks for security.

Limitations:
- Free VPNs may have speed/data limitations.
- VPNs do not protect against phishing or malware.
- Some websites block traffic from known VPN IPs.
- Can slightly reduce browsing speed due to encryption overhead.
